Bubble Shooter- Tripple Smash

It's a bubble shooting game made of HTML, JavaScript.

Link:

https://rajspeaks.github.io/bubble-shooter

Instructions:

  1. Must be atleast 3 balls of same colour to get smashed with each other.
  2. For each smashing, there is 300 points.
  3. While striking the balls to upward direction, if any of the lines or segments of balls (Either be vertically or horizontlly or diagonally) get in touch with the bottom line, you will be disqualified.
  4. Carefully notice the colour of the balls provided below before hitting.
